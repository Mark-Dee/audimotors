Our Story

Welcome to Audi motors international, where our passion for Audi cars drives everything we do. Founded in 2019, we set out with a clear mission: to provide Audi enthusiasts with the ultimate car-buying experience. Our journey began when Donald Ochieng, a lifelong Audi aficionado, envisioned a dealership that not only offered the latest models but also fostered a community of Audi enthusiasts. With a small team of dedicated car enthusiasts, we opened our doors with a commitment to excellence and a love for the Audi brand.

Over the years, we've grown into a premier destination for Audi lovers, offering a wide range of Audi models, from the sleek and sporty A3 to the luxurious Q7. But our commitment to customer satisfaction remains unchanged. We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers, providing personalized service, and ensuring that every visit to our dealership is memorable.

Today, as we look back on our journey, we are proud of how far we've come. But our story is far from over. We continue to innovate and evolve, always striving to exceed our customers' expectations and remain at the forefront of the Audi experience. Join us at Audi motors international and experience the thrill of driving an Audi like never before. Welcome to the future of Audi.
